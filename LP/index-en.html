Features

What Setris helps you do

Manage live schedules
with setlists and rehearsals

Keep upcoming shows, rehearsals, and past performance history together. Quickly check dates, venues, doors, and start times when you need them.

Organize setlists, members, and reservation lists per live show. Access what you need during preparation and on the day of the show.

Register bands and songs
for multiple projects

Save bands, songs, and venues in advance. Reduce repeated input when creating live schedules and keep multiple bands organized.

Store key, BPM, and score links for each song. Keep song information by band and build setlists more smoothly. You can also add links to external services such as YouTube and Spotify, so songs are easy to play right away.

Review live history
and frequently played songs

Keep finished shows as activity records. Review show counts, reservation trends, and frequently played songs for future planning.

Reports also show song performance trends and band-by-band history, making your activity easy to look back on.

Q&A

Which operating systems are supported? Can I use it on an older iPhone?

Setris supports iOS 18 and later. Android is not supported at this time. The app is very lightweight at about 5 MB, and quick operation is one of its strengths.

Will it slow down if I register many live shows?

Registration and browsing have been stress-tested with 7,000 dummy live-show records. Setris is designed with long-term use and growing data volume in mind.

Do I need to register personal information?

Setris does not collect any personal information. You can start using it right away without creating an account.

Can I back up my data?

Yes. Text data such as live shows, songs, bands, and venues can be backed up simply as JSON. Flyer images, PDFs, app settings, and announcement templates are not included.

Are more features planned?

A stage-plot creation feature is under consideration. Other feature requests are welcome.
